MultiPIT

Multi-Topic Paraphrases in Twitter

 

ABOUT

MultiPIT is a corpus of paraphrases from Twitter for paraphrase identification. It contains two parts: MultiPITcrowd and MultiPITexpert. MultiPITcrowd is a collection of crowdsourcing annoations with loosely defined paraphrase definitions. MultiPITexpert is a collection of expert annotations with strict defined paraphrase definitions. MultiPIT-Auto is a collection of automatically identified paraphrases pairs from recent Twitter data. The work is by natural language processing (NLP) researchers from the Georgia Institute of Technology
